Light and dark. Protagonist and antagonist. The villains and heroes of B10nica were no different from the usual script. The eternal conflict between the two sides forced the evolution of several unique characteristics shared between them and an "Essence" to guide their vessel; the B10nic. Hidden behind infinite layers of metamorphosed cells, a B10nic’s true essence was previously invisible to the naked eye. However, with the creation of the so called Metaverse, a shift in the paradigm was inevitable...

Now, a B10nic’s essence radiates through several disruptions of its body, creating a power system between the ranks.

Roadmap

Being a part of the MetaB10nic project automatically creates a division between the so called holders. Both however have the same ultimate goal of rebuilding B10nica. Have it be Decentraland, Sandbox or even NFT Worlds, the options are limitless, but shall be decided upon with the creation of the B10nic council [DAO]. $tokens being the national currency of our promised land, the economy of B10nica shall thrive through trade in its very own markets and festivals.
