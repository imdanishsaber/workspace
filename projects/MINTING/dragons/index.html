Rewards

The objective of the game is to collect tokens while running with your minted Dragon NFT.

Avoid hitting the obstacles by moving your dragons left or right, sliding down or jumping over the obstacles or else the game will be over. The more time you run without hitting an obstacle, the more tokens you will get.

Depending on the rarity your Dragon NFT, you may get 100 tokens to a maximum of 1000 tokens daily as rewards.

Don’t miss out on the daily rewards and play daily to gather more tokens. These tokens can be used to mint more Dragon NFT in the marketplace.

Rewards are automatically distributed to the Dragon NFT owner within a 24-hour timeframe.
