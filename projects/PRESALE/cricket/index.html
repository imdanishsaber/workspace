About Cricket token

There are many possibilities. Today, the cricketing world is
split across several geographies, several ecosystem partners,
and its value is not analyzed fully. The fans, players, and clubs
cannot interact globally easily due to several difficulties
ranging from operational, technical, contractual, etc. Cricket
makes everything seamless globally.

The Cricket token brings together the Cricketing world. For all
use cases from Digital Collectibles, Ticketing, Merchandising,
Fantasy Sports, eSports, Licensing, etc A place for the future
of the sport
