FAQ

How does our Platform works?
Our platform operates by having investors invest in it. Once invested, they can earn a daily ROI of up to 2-5% on their investments. They can choose to reinvest their earnings daily, and collect their earnings after a period of time. After collection, they can withdraw their earnings within 24 hours. All the investments in the total value locked (TVL) are securely traded on decentralized exchanges, generating income to sustain and extend the lifespan of the TVL.
Will my ROI still be 2% if I make a deposit after 15 or 45 days?
No, the timing of the investment is based solely on the initial investment. For example, if you invested in the platform 100 days ago and then invest again after another 100 days, your ROI will still be 5%.
How is the project facilitating decentralized trades?
When the chart of a particular coin is on an upward trend, we will initiate a trade execution process. This involves purchasing the token with BNB and transferring it to our smart contract automatically without owner access. Next, we set a limit for selling the token once it reaches a certain value, which will trigger an automatic sale of the token for profit. The profit, along with any BNB earned, will be directed back to our smart contract.
Will the owner be holding the funds for trading?
No, all trading is automated through our smart contract. We do not hold any investor funds, and can only execute trades on decentralized exchanges without gaining access to the investor funds.
Has the team undergone KYC (Know Your Customer) verification and are they doxxed?
We are not holding any funds belonging to investors. However, if needed we will do it.
Is DexFactory a Ponzi scheme?
No, it's actually a sound business model that relies on decentralized trades. This involves executing trades on decentralized exchanges in order to generate income for the total value locked (TVL).
How do the Limits works?
DexFactory is a legitimate business model, not a Ponzi scheme. Investing in it incurs a 6% deposit fee and 4% referral fee, reducing your total investment to 0.9 BNB and potentially affecting your maximum withdrawal limit. By following the 6/1 strategy and continuously reinvesting your earnings, you will be able to increase your initial investment and withdrawal limit
Is there any future for DexFactory?
DexFactory has a very bright future because it is an innovative idea that facilitates decentralized trades, ultimately increasing the total value locked (TVL). Additionally, we plan to launch our token in September, following the success of DexFactory.
Can you explain how the rewards and withdrawals work?
If you invest 1 Binance Coin (BNB) as a new user on our platform, you will receive a 2% return on investment (ROI) over a period of 1 to 15 days. This means that you will receive a daily reward of 0.02 BNB (calculated as 1/100 * 2 = 0.02 BNB).After every 24 hours, your rewards will be added to your investment, and the next 48 hours will bring an increased reward of 0.04 BNB. You have the option to either reinvest your rewards, which will further increase your investment, or collect your reward, which will be added to your withdraw amount. Once you collect your reward, you can withdraw the total amount available to you every 24 hours.
Does the owner receive the fees applied on unstaked funds?
No, the fees directly goes to the DexFactory TVL.
What is the timeline for processing each withdrawal?
Basically, you have 24 hours to withdraw your rewards, regardless of when you collected them. If you collected your rewards early and your withdrawal time has arrived, you can withdraw them. However, if you miss your withdrawal time, You can still withdraw but you will get 'new time' for the upcoming withdraw.
What is difference between 'Re-Invest' and 'Collect'
If you choose to re-invest your daily rewards, they will be added to your original investment, resulting in an increase in your daily earnings and overall limit. On the other hand, if you decide to collect your earnings, they will be transferred to your 'Withdraw Amount' account, from where you can withdraw them within 24 hours.
Will I receive a direct referral bonus if I invite someone?
Yes, you will get the referral bonus directly
